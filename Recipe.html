Recipe

Chips

Ingredients:

1kg of Russet Potatos

Directions:

1.Slice potatoes into French fries, and place into cold water so they won't turn brown while you prepare the oil.

2.Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.

3.Place in the hot oil so they are not touching at first. The fries must be placed into the skillet one at a time, or they will clump together. Fry until golden brown and crispy. Remove and drain on paper towels.

Soup

Ingredients:

2tbsp olive oil,1 onion chopped,2 large red peppers choped,2 cloves of garlic chopped,600g tomatoes chopped, 1tin canned tomotoes, 1L chicken stock, 1tbsp Fresh basil

Instruction:

1.Heat the olive oil in a large saucepan. Saute the onion, peppers, and garlic for about 5 minutes. Add the fresh tomatoes and cook for another 3-4 minutes.

2.Add the tinned tomatoes, the stock and 1 (250ml) cup of water. Bring to the boil, reduce the heat and simmer for 30 minutes.

3.Remove from the heat and allow to cool a little. Puree the soup in batches in a food processor or with a hand-held blender. Strain through a coarse sieve.

Recipe

Pasta

Ingredients:

Pasta, Sauce, Filling

Intructions:

1.Only partially cook dry pasta. In a pot of abundantly salted boiling water, cook dry pasta for only half or three-quarters of the recommended time.

2.Add the semi-cooked pasta to a pan sauce. Using a slotted spoon (for shaped pasta) or tongs (for spaghetti), remove the partially cooked pasta from the water, reserving the water for later use. Add the pasta to the pan of sauce.

3.Adjust the thickness of the sauce by adding the reserved water. Finish cooking the pasta in the cuisson.

Recipe

Ice-Cream

Ingredients:

2 cups cold heavy/whipping cream, 1 can sweetened condensed milk, Your choice of mix-ins

Instructions:

1.Whip cream with mixer or whisk until stiff peaks form. Make sure you use cream straight from the fridge as it will whip faster

2.Pour sweetened condensed milk into large bowl and stir in your desired flavorings and mix-ins.

3.Gently fold the whipped cream into the milk mixture until all combined.

Recipe

Tiramisu

Ingredients:

6 eggs, separated, 220 g (1 cup) caster sugar, 500 g mascarpone, 400 g packet pavesini biscuits

Instructions:

1.Beat the egg yolks and the sugar for at least 15 minutes, or until thick and white. Add the mascarpone and beat until just combined but smooth.

2.Beat the eggwhites until thick and stiff, then gently fold into the mascarpone mixture.

3.Layer the biscuits and mascarpone cream in a large glass bowl or individual glass bowls. Refrigerate for at least 2-3 hours. Before serving, top with a generous grating of chocolate.
